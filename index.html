About Our Team

Obalduyes is a First Lego League team focused on thoughtful design, clear communication, measurable progress, good ideas and realizations.

What is FLL?

First Lego League (FLL) is an inspiring robotics program where teams solve real-world challenges through research, building, and presenting their solutions.

Our goals and values

  • Design with purpose, test with discipline.
  • Communicate clearly and respectfully.
  • Improve through iteration and feedback.
